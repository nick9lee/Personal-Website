Personal Projects

Tic Tac Toe

A text base version of tic tac toe that features 4 different ways of playing. Players can choose to play another human, a player that picks a random empty spot, a player that checks if the other player must be blocked before picking a random empty spot on the board, and a player that actively tries to get three in a row while looking out for if the opposition must be blocked. This really helped me cement my understanding of OOP.

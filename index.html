Tic Tac Toe

A text base version of tic tac toe that feature 4 different ways of playing. Players can choose to play another human, a player that picks a random empty spot, a player that checks if the other player must be blocked before picking a random empty spot on the board, and a player that actively tries to get three in a row while looking out for if the opposition must be blocked.
